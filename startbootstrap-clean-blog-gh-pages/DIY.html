  1. Why it's scenic?-Griffith Observatory:
    • There is a great view of the city of Los Angeles.
    • his is one of the best spots to see the Hollywood sign.
    • From the terraces you can get a great view of the beautiful sunsets.
  2. Why it's scenic?-Santa Catalina Island:
    • When on a high elevation you can get a view of the beautiful coastaltowns.
    • There are many hikes where you can see the wonderful rolling hills and landscape of the island.
    • The view of the aqua blue color and the white sand is so unique.
  3. Why it's scenic?-Malibu:
    • There a incredible views as you drive down the canyon roads.
    • The beaches are an amazing place to watch the colorful sunsets.
    • The canyons have hikes with breathtaking views of waterfalls and the ocean.
