Joanne's Favorite Dishes

Pozole

    This is a Mexican soup packed with flavorful ingredients that I grew up loving. It is often served as a celebratory dish on New Year's Eve. This is one of my favorite dishes because of the delicious broth, meat, and vegetables that get their unique flavor from the rich spices and chiles added. Pozole Recipe

Ceviche Tostadas

    This is a citrusy seafood dish that is popular throughout Latin America. It is traditionally made by cooking raw shrimp in lemon juice. This is one of my favorite dishes because I love seafood especially when the mix of flavors includes fresh lemon juice and when there is a range of textures. Ceviche Tostadas Recipe

Guatemalan Tamales

    This is a traditional Guatemalan dish that is eaten on special occasions especially on Christmas and New Year's. This is one my favorite dishes because my family and I have bonded every December when we make them together. I have many fond memories of gathering with my family and enjoying them in eachothers company on Christmas. Guatemalan Tamales Recipe
